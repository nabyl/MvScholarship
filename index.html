Mv Scholarship (Maldives)

This is an android application which scans the Department of Higher Education website and displays all the scholarships listing.

About App

This app was developed to give any of the Maldivian students an easy access to the scholarship listings provided by the Department of Higher Education, Maldives. The listing and content displayed on the app is solely owned by the DHE.

Features

  • Listing of Scholarships
  • PDF Viewing implemented

Enhancements Planned

  • Notify user when a new listing is added.
